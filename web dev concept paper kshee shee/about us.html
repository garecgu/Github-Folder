TEAM KIM AND FRENDS


Kim and Friends are a group of Computer Science students who made this website as a suggestion specifically for KCC Grocery. They felt it was convenient to create this website especially at this time of pandemic. Their goal towards making this website is for people who want to prepare what they want to buy in advance and for other people who may have special dietary needs as they can easily check the items available the website provides.
