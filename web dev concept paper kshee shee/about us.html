KCC FOUNDERS


KCC Malls is owned by Korondal Commercial Corporation (KCC) with their headquarters at General Santos City. KCC Malls and its entire department is owned and managed by KCC Property Holdings Inc.


KCC HISTORY


In 1947, KCC started as a textile store in Koronadal City. Overtime KCC earned the title KCC Shoping Center as they expanded as a supermarket and department store in 1989. Slowly but surely they expanded their business as they open a second branch in General Santos City in 1992 as KCC Warehouse Plaza or later known as KCC Mall of Gensan in 1996. 2000 is where they renamed their first store in Koronadal City as KCC Mall of Marbel. Eventually in 2015 and 2018, they now opened their business at Zamboanga City and South Cotabato City respectively.

Now, KCC Malls is the leading shopping destination in South Central Mindanao as it is growing in one the fastest growing cities, such as: General Santos, Koronadal, and Zamboanga City.
